# What is Dynamic Ad Insertion

**Dynamic Ad Insertion (DAI) is a digital advertising technology that replaces generic ad slots in pre-recorded audio or video content with targeted, up-to-date advertisements at the moment of playback.**

Unlike traditional methods where ads are "baked in" during production, DAI uses a server-based system to stitch ads into the content dynamically. When a listener presses play on a podcast episode, the hosting platform identifies key information (like the user's location, device, or listening habits) and requests a specific ad from an ad server in real-time. This ad is then seamlessly inserted into the designated ad break. This allows a single podcast episode to feature different advertisements for different listeners, or even for the same listener at different times.

The process relies on metadata markers placed within the audio file by the podcaster or hosting platform. These markers indicate where ad breaks are located. When the playback reaches a marker, the dynamic insertion occurs. This technology is fundamental to modern podcast monetization and is also widely used in streaming radio and video-on-demand services.

**Why it matters:** DAI revolutionizes podcast advertising by making it more relevant, flexible, and measurable. Advertisers can target specific audiences, run time-sensitive campaigns (like promoting a weekend sale), and track performance more accurately. For podcasters, it means the ability to monetize back catalog episodes with fresh ads, maximizing revenue from every listen. Listeners benefit from more relevant ads, and the overall listening experience can be improved with localized or updated messages.

**Use cases:** A national retailer can target podcast ads only to listeners in regions where they have stores. A podcast about technology can feature ads for a new software launch that are only inserted during the two-week launch period, even in episodes published months ago. A podcast host can record a generic ad slot like "We'll be right back after this message," and DAI will populate it with a relevant ad for each unique listener.
